ZYA Preloved is an independent business with the idea of a store that sells preowned clothes. Nur Alya Natasha, our product's head manager, is led by two other colleagues, Alia Natasya and Zameer Fayyad. We welcome people of all sizes, allowing them to express their individual sense of aesthetic fashion however they see match.
Our clothing products are primarily shirts with various fashion designs, though we may also offer knitwear, jackets, and blazers in a similar vein. At ZYA Preloved, we always make sure that the materials used to make our products are real and not the result of any dubious transactions, like black-market purchases or other related schemes.
